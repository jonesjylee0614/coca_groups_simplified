Chen **wanted** to **make** a **difference** in the **world**. But he **didn't** **know** **where** to **start**. **Every** **problem** **seemed** too **big**, **every** **challenge** too **complex**.

"I **want** to **help**," he **told** his **grandfather**, "but I'm just one **person**. **What** **difference** can I **make**?"

His **grandfather** **smiled**. "You **want** to **know** the **secret** of **making** a **difference**?"

Chen **nodded** **eagerly**.

"**Start** **small**," his **grandfather** **said**. "**Make** a **difference** for **one** **person**. Then **another**. Then **another**. **Before** you **know** it, you've **changed** **many** **lives**."

Chen **decided** to **try**. **Every** **day**, he'd **do** **something** **small** to **help** **someone**. He'd **help** a **neighbor** **carry** **groceries**. He'd **volunteer** at a **local** **shelter**. He'd **listen** to a **friend** who **needed** to **talk**.

**Each** **action** was **small**. But **together**, they **made** a **difference**. **People** **started** **noticing**. "You're **always** **helping**," they'd **say**. "You **make** things **better**."

Chen **realized** his **grandfather** was **right**. You **didn't** **need** to **change** the **whole** **world** **at** **once**. You just **needed** to **start** **making** a **difference** **where** you **were**, with **what** you **had**.

"I **want** to **keep** **making** a **difference**," Chen **told** his **grandfather** **months** **later**. "**Every** **day**, in **small** **ways**."

His **grandfather** **hugged** him. "That's **how** **real** **change** **happens**. **One** **person**, **one** **act** of **kindness**, **one** **difference** at a **time**."
