At seventy years **old**, Professor **Lee** was **still** **learning**. **Even** **though** he'd **spent** **fifty** years **teaching**, he **believed** there was **always** **more** to **learn**.

"**Although** I'm **old**," he'd **tell** his **students**, "I'm **still** a **student** **myself**. **Learning** **never** **stops**."

One **young** **student** **asked**: "But **even** at your **age**, you're **still** **learning**? **Don't** you **know** **everything** by **now**?"

Professor **Lee** **laughed**. "**Although** I've **learned** **much**, I **know** **very** **little**. The **more** I **learn**, the **more** I **realize** **how** **much** I **don't** **know**."

He **explained**: "**Even** **if** I **lived** a **thousand** **years**, I **couldn't** **learn** **everything**. The **world** is **too** **vast**, **too** **complex**, **too** **wonderful** to **ever** **fully** **understand**."

The **students** were **inspired**. **Although** they were **young**, they **realized** they **shouldn't** **stop** **learning** when they **graduated**. **Even** **after** **school**, **learning** should **continue**.

"**Even** **though** you're **still** **young**," Professor **Lee** **told** them, "**start** **developing** a **love** for **learning** **now**. **Although** **formal** **education** **ends**, **real** **learning** is **lifelong**."

Years **later**, his **students** **still** **remembered** this **lesson**. **Even** when they **became** **experts** in their **fields**, they **remained** **students** at **heart**. **Although** they **knew** **much**, they **still** **sought** to **learn** **more**.

"Professor **Lee** **taught** us," one **student** **said**, "that **even** **though** we **grow** **older**, we **should** **still** **stay** **curious**. **Although** we **gain** **knowledge**, we **should** **still** **ask** **questions**."

That was Professor **Lee**'s **greatest** **gift**: **teaching** his **students** that **learning** was not a **destination** but a **journey** that **continued** **even** **into** **old** **age**.
