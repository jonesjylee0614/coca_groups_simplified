"**Action** speaks **louder** than **words**," Coach **Johnson** **always** **said**. His **team** **knew** he **meant** it.

The **player** **understood** now. **Talk** was **cheap**. **Action** was **valuable**. If you **wanted** to **achieve** **something**, you had to **take** **action**, not just **talk** about it.
