**Speak** up. **Use** your **voice**. Don't **remain** **silent** when you **see** **injustice**. This was the **message** **activist** **Rosa** **Martinez** **taught** her **community**.

"Your **voice** **matters**," she'd **say**. "When you **speak**, you **give** **voice** to **others** who **can't** **speak** for **themselves**."

For **years**, **Rosa** had been the **voice** for the **voiceless**. She'd **speak** **at** **meetings**, **raise** her **voice** **against** **unfairness**, **use** her **voice** to **demand** **change**.

"But I'm just one **voice**," **someone** would **often** **say**. "What **difference** can one **voice** **make**?"

"**Every** **great** **movement** **started** with one **voice**," **Rosa** would **reply**. "That one **voice** **inspired** **another**, then **another**. **Soon** **many** **voices** **joined** **together**. That's how **change** **happens**."

She **taught** her **community** to **find** their **voice**. "Your **voice** is your **power**," she'd **explain**. "Don't **let** **fear** **silence** your **voice**. Don't **let** **doubt** **quiet** your **words**."

**Young** **people** **especially** **listened**. They'd been **told** to **stay** **quiet**, to **not** **make** **waves**. But **Rosa** **taught** them **different**: "**Raise** your **voice**. **Speak** your **truth**. **Use** your **voice** for **good**."

Her **influence** **grew**. **More** **people** **found** their **voice**. **More** **voices** **spoke** up **together**. The **chorus** of **voices** became **impossible** to **ignore**.

"One **voice** can **start** a **conversation**," **Rosa** would **say**. "Many **voices** can **start** a **movement**. Your **voice** **matters**. **Never** **forget** that."
