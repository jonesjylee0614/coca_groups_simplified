## Part 5. 语法聚焦 (Grammar Focus)

### 聚焦点 1: "so + be/助动词 + 主语" 倒装结构

**原句:** "The range of human suffering is vast, but so is our capacity for healing."

**聚焦:** "so + be动词 + 主语" 倒装结构

**用法说明:**
这个结构用于表示"...也是如此",用来避免重复前面提到的形容词或状态。结构为:
- so + be动词/助动词/情态动词 + 主语

这种倒装让句子更简洁,同时强调了相似性。

**例句:**
- She is happy, and so am I.
- He can swim, and so can his brother.
- They were tired, and so were we.

**对比:**
- 倒装: She is smart, and so is he. (简洁)
- 不倒装: She is smart, and he is smart too. (重复)

**可替换句:** "The range of human suffering is vast, but our capacity for healing is also vast."

---

### 聚焦点 2: "no longer" 的用法

**原句:** "She was no longer just analyzing data."

**聚焦:** "no longer" 表示"不再"

**用法说明:**
"no longer" 是较正式的表达,意为"不再",通常放在:
1. be动词之后: She is no longer a student.
2. 助动词之后: He can no longer walk.
3. 实义动词之前: They no longer live here.

"no longer" 比 "not...anymore" 更正式,常用于书面语。

**对比:**
- 正式: She no longer works there. (书面)
- 口语: She doesn't work there anymore. (口语)

**可替换句:** "She wasn't just analyzing data anymore."
