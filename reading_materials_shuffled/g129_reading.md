## Part 1. Story (英文原文)

Commander Sarah Chen floated **across** /əˈkrɔːs/ (prep. 穿过；横跨) the observation deck of the space station, her eyes fixed on Earth below. The **blue** /bluː/ (adj. 蓝色的) planet hung in the **black** /blæk/ (adj. 黑色的) void of space, a fragile marble suspended in nothing. She moved **quickly** /ˈkwɪkli/ (adv. 快速地) to the control panel—another alert from the **academic** /ˌækəˈdemɪk/ (adj. 学术的) research team.

"Dr. Martinez," she called to the mission's chief scientist, "the **orbit** /ˈɔːrbɪt/ (n. 轨道) calculations are complete. We're in **position** /pəˈzɪʃn/ (n. 位置；职位) for the **sector** /ˈsektər/ (n. 部门；扇区) scan."

The mission was unprecedented—not just exploration, but a search for signs of intelligent life. The **investment** /ɪnˈvestmənt/ (n. 投资) from multiple nations had been enormous, and the pressure on the crew was intense. As an **agent** /ˈeɪdʒənt/ (n. 代理人；特工) of humanity's curiosity, Sarah felt the weight of billions watching from below.

Dr. Martinez pulled himself **across** /əˈkrɔːs/ (prep. 穿过) the module, a **sheet** /ʃiːt/ (n. 床单；纸张) of data in his hand. "The readings are **spell**ing /ˈspelɪŋ/ (v. 拼写；招致) out something interesting," he said. "There's an anomaly in **sector** /ˈsektər/ (n. 扇区) Seven. Not debris—something with a clear pattern."

Sarah's pulse **quickly** /ˈkwɪkli/ (adv. 快速地) accelerated. "**Being** /ˈbiːɪŋ/ (n. 存在；生物) from another world?" she asked, half-joking but half-hopeful.

"Maybe. Or **being** /ˈbiːɪŋ/ (v. 是) honest, it could be nothing. But we need to investigate. The **map** /mæp/ (n. 地图) coordinates put it about two million kilometers from our current **position** /pəˈzɪʃn/ (n. 位置)."

The decision was **quickly** /ˈkwɪkli/ (adv. 迅速地) made. They would **pass** /pæs/ (v. 通过；传递) through the region during their next **orbit** /ˈɔːrbɪt/ (n. 轨道), gathering data. Sarah felt **none** /nʌn/ (pron. 没有一个) of the fear she'd expected—only excitement. This was why she'd become an astronaut, why she'd spent **late** /leɪt/ (adj. 晚的；已故的) nights studying, training, pushing her limits.

"**Provided** /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ (conj. 假如；倘若) that the **gear** /ɡɪr/ (n. 装备；齿轮) holds up," noted Engineer Lopez, always the cautious one, "we should have **none** /nʌn/ (det. 没有) of the technical issues that plagued the last mission."

The hours **pass**ed /pæst/ (v. 过去) slowly as they approached the coordinates. Sarah reviewed the mission **complaint** /kəmˈpleɪnt/ (n. 投诉；抱怨) logs from previous flights—**none** /nʌn/ (pron. 没有一个) had encountered anything like this. The **destruction** /dɪˈstrʌkʃn/ (n. 破坏；毁灭) of certainty was both thrilling and terrifying.

"**Game** /ɡeɪm/ (n. 游戏；猎物) time," announced Lopez, checking instruments. "We're entering the **sector** /ˈsektər/ (n. 部门) now."

The monitors lit up with data. The **sheet** /ʃiːt/ (n. 表格) of readings showed electromagnetic patterns **you** /juː/ (pron. 你；你们) wouldn't expect from natural phenomena. Sarah's training as an **academic** /ˌækəˈdemɪk/ (n. 学者) kicked in—she began logging observations with precision.

"This could **convey** /kənˈveɪ/ (v. 传达；运送) information," Dr. Martinez said, his voice trembling with excitement. "**Provided** /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ (conj. 假如) we're interpreting correctly, these patterns repeat. It's like... like someone **spell**ing /ˈspelɪŋ/ (v. 拼写) out a message in light."

Sarah's mind raced. "Can **you** /juː/ (pron. 你) decode it?"

"Not yet. But the fact that there's a pattern **except** /ɪkˈsept/ (prep. 除了) random noise—that's significant. Someone, or something, wanted to be found."

The **game** /ɡeɪm/ (n. 博弈) had changed. This wasn't just exploration; it was first contact. Sarah thought about the **divorce** /dɪˈvɔːrs/ (n. 离婚；分离) from Earth they'd experienced leaving the planet. Out here, humanity's problems seemed small. Political **complaint**s /kəmˈpleɪnts/ (n. 抱怨), social divisions—**none** /nʌn/ (det. 没有) of it mattered when faced with the vast unknown.

"We need to **convey** /kənˈveɪ/ (v. 传达) this to Earth Control **quickly** /ˈkwɪkli/ (adv. 快速地)," Sarah decided. "**Provided** /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ (conj. 如果) that the signal reaches them, they'll want to adjust our **orbit** /ˈɔːrbɪt/ (n. 轨道) for extended observation."

As they transmitted the data, Sarah floated **across** /əˈkrɔːs/ (prep. 越过) to the observation window again. The **destruction** /dɪˈstrʌkʃn/ (n. 毁灭) of isolation—the realization that humanity might not be alone—**hurt** /hɜːrt/ (v. 伤害；疼痛) in a strange way. It challenged everything **you** /juː/ (pron. 你；人们) thought **you** /juː/ (pron. 你) knew about your **position** /pəˈzɪʃn/ (n. 地位) in the universe.

**Except** /ɪkˈsept/ (conj. 除非；但是) it also healed something. The petty conflicts, the reasons for **divorce** /dɪˈvɔːrs/ (n. 分离) and division among humans—they all seemed absurd now. If there were others out there, what would **you** /juː/ (pron. 你) say? What would **you** /juː/ (pron. 你) **convey** /kənˈveɪ/ (v. 表达) about humanity?

"Commander," Dr. Martinez called, "Earth Control is responding. They're authorizing an extended mission. And they want us to attempt communication. To **spell** /spel/ (v. 拼出) out a response using our transmitters."

Sarah nodded. "What should we say?"

Lopez laughed. "**Provided** /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ (conj. 假如) that anyone's actually listening, maybe just start with 'hello'?"

They worked for hours, crafting a simple **map** /mæp/ (n. 图表) of electromagnetic pulses that would **convey** /kənˈveɪ/ (v. 传达) basic mathematical concepts—a universal language. The **sheet** /ʃiːt/ (n. 纸张) of transmission protocols grew as they refined their approach. **None** /nʌn/ (pron. 没有人) of them slept, too captivated by the **game** /ɡeɪm/ (n. 游戏) of first contact.

**Late** /leɪt/ (adv. 晚) that night—or what passed for night in space—Sarah recorded a personal log. "We're in a unique **position** /pəˈzɪʃn/ (n. 位置)," she said. "The **investment** /ɪnˈvestmənt/ (n. 投资) wasn't just money or technology. It was humanity's **being** /ˈbiːɪŋ/ (n. 存在)—our desire to reach **across** /əˈkrɔːs/ (prep. 穿越) the void. Whatever **pass**es /ˈpæsɪz/ (v. 发生) next, we've proven we're ready to meet the unknown."

The response came three days **late**r /ˈleɪtər/ (adv. 后来). A pattern emerged—complex, beautiful, unmistakable. Someone had answered. Sarah felt tears form, floating away in the zero gravity like tiny **blue** /bluː/ (adj. 蓝色的) jewels.

"They heard us," she whispered. "**Across** /əˈkrɔːs/ (prep. 跨越) millions of kilometers, through the **destruction** /dɪˈstrʌkʃn/ (n. 破坏) of distance, they heard us."

Dr. Martinez was reading the decoded **sheet** /ʃiːt/ (n. 清单). "It's mathematical. Coordinates. They're showing us a **map** /mæp/ (n. 地图) to their **sector** /ˈsektər/ (n. 区域). And... this part **spell**s /spelz/ (v. 拼写) out something. I think it means 'friend' or 'peace'."

Lopez checked the **gear** /ɡɪr/ (n. 装备). "We have enough fuel and life support to **pass** /pæs/ (v. 通过) through that region. The **orbit** /ˈɔːrbɪt/ (n. 轨道) adjustment would take us there in about six months."

Sarah made the decision that would define her career, perhaps humanity's future. "**Except** /ɪkˈsept/ (prep. 除了) for basic necessities, divert all resources to the journey. This **investment** /ɪnˈvestmənt/ (n. 投资) is worth it. **None** /nʌn/ (det. 没有) of the **complaint**s /kəmˈpleɪnts/ (n. 抱怨) from Earth can **hurt** /hɜːrt/ (v. 伤害) what we're about to accomplish."

As the station **quickly** /ˈkwɪkli/ (adv. 快速地) adjusted course, Sarah thought about the message they'd sent. **Being** /ˈbiːɪŋ/ (v. 作为) humanity's **agent** /ˈeɪdʒənt/ (n. 代表) meant showing the best of what **you** /juː/ (pron. 你们) were. The **game** /ɡeɪm/ (n. 博弈) of first contact wasn't about winning—it was about understanding.

**Provided** /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ (conj. 只要) that they approached with respect and curiosity, this encounter could **convey** /kənˈveɪ/ (v. 传达) hope rather than fear. The **destruction** /dɪˈstrʌkʃn/ (n. 毁灭) of old certainties made room for new possibilities. And **none** /nʌn/ (pron. 没有什么) **except** /ɪkˈsept/ (prep. 除了) courage could **pass** /pæs/ (v. 通过) them through what came next.

## Part 2. 故事翻译 (中文)

陈莎拉指挥官飘浮着穿过空间站的观察甲板,她的眼睛盯着下方的地球。这颗蓝色的星球悬挂在太空的黑色虚空中,一颗脆弱的弹珠悬浮在虚无中。她快速移动到控制面板——学术研究团队发来了另一个警报。

"马丁内斯博士,"她叫道,这是任务的首席科学家,"轨道计算完成了。我们在扇区扫描的位置上。"

这次任务是前所未有的——不仅仅是探索,而是寻找智慧生命的迹象。来自多个国家的投资是巨大的,对船员的压力也很大。作为人类好奇心的代理人,莎拉感受到数十亿人从下面观看的重量。

马丁内斯博士拉着自己穿过舱室,手里拿着一张数据表。"读数正在招致一些有趣的东西,"他说。"第七扇区有异常。不是碎片——是具有清晰模式的东西。"

莎拉的脉搏快速加快。"来自另一个世界的生物?"她问,半开玩笑但半怀希望。

"也许。或者诚实地说,可能什么都不是。但我们需要调查。地图坐标显示它距离我们当前位置约200万公里。"

决定很快就做出了。他们将在下一次轨道期间通过该区域,收集数据。莎拉没有感到她预期的恐惧——只有兴奋。这就是她成为宇航员的原因,为什么她花了很晚的夜晚学习、训练、突破极限。

"假如装备能撑住,"工程师洛佩兹指出,总是谨慎的那个,"我们应该没有困扰上次任务的技术问题。"

当他们接近坐标时,时间过得很慢。莎拉查看了以前飞行的任务投诉日志——没有一个遇到过这样的事情。确定性的毁灭既令人兴奋又令人恐惧。

"游戏时间,"洛佩兹宣布,检查仪器。"我们现在正在进入该部门。"

监视器亮起数据。读数表显示你不会从自然现象中预期的电磁模式。莎拉作为学者的训练开始了——她开始精确地记录观察结果。

"这可能传达信息,"马丁内斯博士说,他的声音因兴奋而颤抖。"假如我们解释正确,这些模式会重复。就像...像有人在用光拼写信息。"

莎拉的思绪飞转。"你能解码吗?"

"还不能。但是除了随机噪音之外还有一个模式这一事实——这很重要。有人,或某物,想要被发现。"

博弈改变了。这不仅仅是探索;这是第一次接触。莎拉想起了他们离开地球时经历的与地球的分离。在这里,人类的问题似乎很小。政治抱怨、社会分歧——面对巨大的未知时,这些都不重要。

"我们需要快速向地球控制中心传达这一点,"莎拉决定。"如果信号到达他们,他们会想调整我们的轨道以进行扩展观察。"

当他们传输数据时,莎拉再次飘浮穿过观察窗。孤立的毁灭——意识到人类可能不是孤独的——以一种奇怪的方式伤害了人。它挑战了你认为你知道的关于你在宇宙中地位的一切。

但它也治愈了一些东西。琐碎的冲突,人类之间分离和分裂的原因——现在看起来都很荒谬。如果那里有其他人,你会说什么?你会表达关于人类的什么?

"指挥官,"马丁内斯博士叫道,"地球控制中心正在回应。他们授权延长任务。他们希望我们尝试通信。使用我们的发射器拼出回应。"

莎拉点点头。"我们应该说什么?"

洛佩兹笑了。"假如真的有人在听,也许就从'你好'开始?"

他们工作了几个小时,制作了一个简单的电磁脉冲图表,可以传达基本的数学概念——一种通用语言。随着他们完善方法,传输协议表不断增长。没有人睡觉,太着迷于第一次接触的游戏。

那天晚些时候——或者在太空中相当于夜晚的时候——莎拉录制了一份个人日志。"我们处于一个独特的位置,"她说。"投资不仅仅是金钱或技术。这是人类的存在——我们跨越虚空的渴望。无论接下来发生什么,我们已经证明我们准备好迎接未知。"

三天后收到了回应。一个模式出现了——复杂、美丽、不容置疑。有人回答了。莎拉感到眼泪形成,在零重力下像微小的蓝色宝石一样飘走。

"他们听到了我们,"她低声说。"跨越数百万公里,穿过距离的破坏,他们听到了我们。"

马丁内斯博士正在阅读解码的清单。"这是数学的。坐标。他们在向我们展示一张通往他们区域的地图。还有...这部分拼写出了什么。我认为这意味着'朋友'或'和平'。"

洛佩兹检查了装备。"我们有足够的燃料和生命支持通过那个区域。轨道调整将在大约六个月内带我们到那里。"

莎拉做出了将定义她的职业生涯,也许是人类未来的决定。"除了基本必需品,将所有资源转移到旅程中。这项投资是值得的。来自地球的抱怨都无法伤害我们即将完成的事情。"

当空间站快速调整航向时,莎拉想起了他们发送的信息。作为人类的代表意味着展示你们最好的一面。第一次接触的博弈不是关于胜利——而是关于理解。

只要他们以尊重和好奇心接近,这次相遇就可以传达希望而不是恐惧。旧确定性的毁灭为新可能性腾出了空间。除了勇气之外,没有什么可以让他们通过接下来发生的事情。

## Part 4. 重点句讲解

### 句子 1
**原句:** "The blue planet hung in the black void of space, a fragile marble suspended in nothing."

**分析:** 同位语"a fragile marble"重新描述"The blue planet",增添诗意。过去分词"suspended in nothing"作后置定语修饰"marble"。整句通过视觉意象传达地球的脆弱和孤独。

**亮点:** "fragile marble"和"suspended in nothing"的组合创造了强烈的孤独感和脆弱感,为故事的宇宙背景设定了哲学基调。

---

### 句子 2
**原句:** "As an agent of humanity's curiosity, Sarah felt the weight of billions watching from below."

**分析:** "As an agent of..."表明身份和责任。"humanity's curiosity"将抽象的探索动机人格化。"the weight of billions"是比喻,将压力具象化为可感知的重量。

**亮点:** 通过"agent of humanity's curiosity"这个表达,将个人探索提升为代表全人类的使命,赋予故事更宏大的意义。

---

### 句子 3
**原句:** "The destruction of isolation—the realization that humanity might not be alone—hurt in a strange way."

**分析:** 破折号用于插入解释性短语。"destruction of isolation"是创造性的表达,将"不再孤独"描述为"孤独的毁灭"。"hurt in a strange way"揭示复杂情感——既是痛苦也是解脱。

**亮点:** 用"destruction"(通常是负面词)描述"不再孤独"(正面结果),展现了发现外星智慧带来的复杂心理冲击。

---

### 句子 4
**原句:** "The game of first contact wasn't about winning—it was about understanding."

**分析:** "game of first contact"是比喻,将外交遭遇类比为游戏。"not about...but about..."对比结构纠正常见误解。从"winning"到"understanding"的转变体现价值观升华。

**亮点:** 通过否定"winning",重新定义"first contact"的本质,传达了和平探索的理念,这是科幻文学中的重要主题。

---

### 句子 5
**原句:** "None except courage could pass them through what came next."

**分析:** 倒装结构"None except courage"强调只有勇气这一品质。"pass them through"中"pass"作及物动词,意为"使通过"。"what came next"是名词性从句,指代未知的未来。

**亮点:** 句末的开放性"what came next"留下悬念,同时"except courage"的强调给读者留下深刻印象,是优秀故事结尾的标志。

## Part 5. 语法聚焦 (Grammar Focus)

### 聚焦点 1: "provided (that)" 引导条件从句

**原句:** "Provided that the gear holds up, we should have none of the technical issues."

**聚焦:** "provided (that)"作为条件连词

**用法说明:**
"provided (that)"意为"假如;倘若;只要",引导条件从句,语气比"if"更正式,常用于书面语或正式场合。它强调某个条件是后续结果的前提。"that"通常可省略。

**与if的区别:**
- if: 更通用,口语书面皆可
- provided (that): 更正式,强调"只要满足这个条件"
- providing (that): 与provided同义,可互换

**时态搭配:**
- 从句通常用现在时表将来
- 主句用will/should/can等情态动词

**其他例子:**
- You can borrow my car, provided you return it by noon. (你可以借我的车,只要中午之前还)
- Provided that we start early, we'll arrive on time. (只要我们早点出发,就能准时到达)

### 聚焦点 2: "none" 的用法

**原句:** "None of the complaints from Earth can hurt what we're about to accomplish."

**聚焦:** "none"作代词和限定词

**用法说明:**
"none"表示"一个也没有;毫无",可作代词或限定词使用。作代词时后接"of + 复数名词/不可数名词",动词可用单数或复数(复数更常见)。"none"比"not any"更强调"完全没有"。

**用法类型:**
1. none of + 复数名词: None of the students passed. (没有一个学生通过)
2. none of + 不可数名词: None of the water is clean. (水一点都不干净)
3. 独立使用: "How many did you see?" "None." (你看到多少个? 一个也没有)

**与no/not any的区别:**
- none: 强调数量为零,可独立使用
- no: 只能作限定词,修饰名词
- not any: 否定但不强调

**其他例子:**
- None of us knew the answer. (我们谁都不知道答案)
- I asked for help, but none was available. (我寻求帮助,但没有可用的)
- None except the brave deserve the reward. (除了勇敢者,没人配得奖励)

**可替换句:** "Not any of the complaints from Earth can hurt..." (用not any,但不如none简洁有力)
