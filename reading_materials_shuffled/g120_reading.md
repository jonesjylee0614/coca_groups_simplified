The first half was **straight** /streɪt/ (adj. 直的；连续的) out of a nightmare. Their rivals dominated, scoring a **touchdown** /ˈtʌtʃdaʊn/ (n. 触地得分) within minutes. The second half began with renewed energy. Marcus refused to **swear** /swer/ (v. 发誓；咒骂) at his teammates when mistakes happened. Instead, he chose to **reply** /rɪˈplaɪ/ (v. 回答) with encouragement. "**Likewise** /ˈlaɪkwaɪz/ (adv. 同样地), we believe in you," they said back. The team's **overall** /ˌoʊvərˈɔːl/ (adj. 总体的) performance improved dramatically.

When reporters asked him to **resume** /rɪˈzuːm/ (v. 恢复；继续) his thoughts on victory, he smiled. "We didn't just **survive** /sərˈvaɪv/ (v. 生存) tonight," he said. ## Part 4. 重点句讲解

### 句子 1
**原句:** "The stadium lights flickered as Marcus stood at the location where champions were born."

**分析:** 这句使用了"as"引导的时间状语从句,同时嵌套了"where"引导的定语从句修饰"location"。"where champions were born"使用被动语态,营造出一种宿命感和历史感,让简单的体育场变得神圣。

**亮点:** 通过定语从句的使用,将普通的地点描写提升到象征意义的层面,这是文学写作中常用的升华技巧。

## Part 5. 语法聚焦 (Grammar Focus)

### 聚焦点 1: 定语从句的使用

**原句:** "The stadium lights flickered as Marcus stood at the location where champions were born."

**聚焦:** 关系副词where引导的定语从句

**用法说明:**
"where"在定语从句中作地点状语,修饰先行词"location"。这种用法常见于描述地点的特殊性质或意义。从句中使用了被动语态"were born",强调地点的象征意义而非具体动作。

**结构分析:**
- 先行词: the location
- 关系副词: where (= at which)
- 从句: champions were born
- 完整表达: at the location at which champions were born

**可替换句:** "Marcus stood at the location that had produced many champions." (使用that引导的定语从句,但意义略有不同)

---

### 聚焦点 2: 比喻结构 (Simile) with "like"

**原句:** "He dodged defenders like water flowing around rocks."

**聚焦:** 明喻的构建方法

**用法说明:**
英语中的明喻通常使用"like"或"as"。本句用"like + 名词 + 现在分词"的结构,创造了生动的视觉画面。现在分词"flowing"作后置定语修饰"water",增加了动态感。

**比喻要素:**
- 本体: He (dodged defenders)
- 喻体: water flowing around rocks
- 相似点: 流畅、自然、不可阻挡

**可替换句:** "He dodged defenders as smoothly as water flows around rocks." (使用as...as结构,更强调程度对比)
