# The Light Chaser: From Small Town to the World
## Complete Chapter Outlines (100 Chapters)

---

## Part 1: Small Town Origins (Chapters 1-10)

### Chapter 1: Family Daily Life (家庭日常生活)
Alex Morgan wakes up in his childhood bedroom for the last time before moving to the city, reflecting on morning routines with his parents at breakfast.

### Chapter 2: Small Town Community (小镇社区)
Alex walks through the familiar streets of Riverside, greeting neighbors and shopkeepers who have known him since childhood.

### Chapter 3: Childhood Memories (童年回忆)
While packing his belongings, Alex discovers old photographs and journals that remind him of growing up in this small Midwestern town.

### Chapter 4: School Education (学校教育)
Alex visits his former high school and meets with Mrs. Peterson, the English teacher who first encouraged his dream of becoming a journalist.

### Chapter 5: Friends and Neighbors (朋友与邻居)
A farewell gathering at the local diner brings together Alex's childhood friends, each pursuing different paths in life.

### Chapter 6: Family Dinner Conversations (家庭餐桌对话)
Over his last family dinner at home, Alex's parents express their concerns and hopes about his move to the big city.

### Chapter 7: Small Town Scenery (小镇风景)
Alex takes a final bike ride through the countryside, visiting the river, the old bridge, and places that shaped his youth.

### Chapter 8: Departure Preparation (离别准备)
With bags packed and goodbyes said, Alex makes final preparations while his mother quietly worries and his father offers practical advice.

### Chapter 9: Dreams and Fears (梦想与恐惧)
On his last night in Riverside, Alex lies awake thinking about his journalism dreams and the uncertainties that lie ahead.

### Chapter 10: The Day of Departure (启程之日)
Alex boards the train to the city while his parents wave goodbye, marking the beginning of his new life.

## Part 2: Arriving in the City (Chapters 11-20)

### Chapter 11: Train Journey (火车旅程)
During the six-hour train ride, Alex watches the landscape change from farmland to suburbs to city skyline while chatting with fellow passengers.

### Chapter 12: First Impressions (第一印象)
Stepping off at Central Station, Alex is overwhelmed by the crowds, noise, and energy of the city that will be his new home.

### Chapter 13: Finding a Place to Live (寻找住处)
Alex spends three exhausting days apartment hunting, discovering that city housing is expensive, cramped, and competitive.

### Chapter 14: City Streets (城市街道)
Lost and confused, Alex learns to navigate the grid system, discovers hidden cafes, and begins to understand the city's rhythm.

### Chapter 15: Job Interviews (求职面试)
Alex attends multiple interviews at newspapers and online media companies, facing rejection but also gaining valuable experience.

### Chapter 16: Coffee Shop Culture (咖啡馆文化)
While working on his laptop in a neighborhood coffee shop, Alex meets other young professionals and begins to build his network.

### Chapter 17: Subway Commute (地铁通勤)
Learning the subway system becomes Alex's daily challenge and education in city life, observing humanity in transit.

### Chapter 18: Kindness from Strangers (陌生人的善意)
When Alex gets lost and his phone dies, an elderly woman helps him find his way, restoring his faith in the city.

### Chapter 19: Homesick Moments (想家时刻)
Alone in his tiny apartment on a rainy evening, Alex calls home and struggles with loneliness and self-doubt.

### Chapter 20: A New Beginning (新的开始)
Alex receives an email offering him a junior reporter position at The Metropolitan Voice, a progressive online news outlet.

### Chapter 26: Interview Skills (采访技巧)
